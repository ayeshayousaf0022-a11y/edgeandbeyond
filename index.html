Frequently Asked Questions

What is Edge & Beyond?

Edge & Beyond makes full-grain leather bags built around one idea: the best quality bag should also be the best organised. Every model is designed with dedicated compartments for your everyday essentials.

How do I get my free Foundation Kit?

As one of our first 50 customers, add any bag to your cart and a gift selection screen will appear automatically, letting you choose your free Foundation Kit item — a leather pouch, a customised leather tag, or a customised leather keychain.

How does checkout work?

Add items to your cart, choose your free gift, then select "Send Order." Fill in your name, phone number and delivery address — your order is emailed directly to our team, who will confirm payment and delivery with you.

Can I change or cancel my order?

Yes — contact us within 12 hours of placing your order via our Contact page and we'll amend or cancel it before it ships.

Is my payment secure?

Yes. All payments are processed through encrypted, PCI-compliant payment providers. We never store your card details on our servers. See "Secure Payment" for details.

Shipping & Delivery

We offer free standard shipping on all orders. Wherever you are, we'll handle the shipping.

Standard delivery typically arrives within 5–7 working days after your order is confirmed. Once dispatched, you'll receive tracking details by email — you can also check progress any time on our Track Order page.

Express shipping options may be available at checkout for select locations.

Returns & Exchanges

Not completely satisfied? You can return your unused item within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Refunds are processed to the original payment method once the returned item has been received and inspected.

To start a return or exchange, contact us via the Contact page with your order details, and we'll send you return instructions and a shipping label.

Items must be unused, in original condition, and with any tags or dust bags included.

Care Instructions

Clean your leather bag with a soft cloth and a suitable leather-safe cleaner. Avoid harsh chemicals, excessive moisture, and prolonged direct sunlight.

Store in a cool, dry place when not in use to preserve its appearance and structure.

Privacy Policy

Edge & Beyond collects only the information needed to process and deliver your order — your name, phone number, delivery address, and order details. This information is sent securely to our order inbox and is never sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Payment information is handled directly by our secure payment provider and is never stored on our website.

You may request a copy of, or the deletion of, any personal data we hold about you by contacting us via the Contact page.
